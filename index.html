Who am I?

Hi! I'm Alex, Co-Owner of IRIDESCENCE LLC- a company of creatively-minded people focusing on making cool indie titles in the mobile spectrum.

There are a plethora of things that I do, firstly being a team and project manager. With a properly developed foundation from day one, I see that whatever project or task assigned is brought to its full completion utilizing SCRUM and WATERFALL tactics.

In my free time I create music and do Foley work for some of my personal projects, and have a very large interest in combining all of this content into a final product. Utilizing software like the Adobe Suite, Blender, FL Studio and Unity, I make my dreams a reality.
